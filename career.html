Internships: Our internship programs offer students hands-on experience in areas like disaster response, public health, communications, and nonprofit management. Through mentorship and networking opportunities, interns gain valuable insights into humanitarian work and prepare for future careers in the field.
Volunteer Opportunities: Students can get involved as volunteers, participating in disaster relief efforts, blood drives, and health and safety training programs. Volunteering provides practical experience and contributes to the Red Cross's mission while helping students develop essential skills for careers in healthcare, emergency services, and public safety.
